Well, It mounted well, however, due to many bad blocks, I get an I/O error and unable to read superblocks errors.

Now, I formatted the remaining 250 GB of wild space into ext 4. (The landlocked space between 100MB ext4 and the 50 GB ntfs partition). And well, burn us a village, the partition mounted successfully.
